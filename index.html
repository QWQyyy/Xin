The long night is coming. I'll watch from now on until I die. I will not marry, I will not confiscate, I will not have children. I will not wear a crown, nor strive for glory. I will do my duty and live and die. I am the sword in the dark, the guard in the Great Wall. I am the iron guard against the cold flame, the light at dawn, the clarion call of the dead, and the guard of the kingdom. I dedicate my life and glory to the night watchman. Tonight is the same, night is the same.
